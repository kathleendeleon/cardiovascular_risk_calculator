# Cardiovascular (CVD) Risk Calculator


A cardiovascular risk score estimator via <br>
-AHA PREVENT (via the published preventr implementation) <br>
-SCORE2 <br>
-and Framingham 2008 (points-based) <br>
 <br> <br>
estimates the 10-year probability of experiencing a heart attack, stroke, or cardiovascular death based on factors like age, sex, blood pressure, cholesterol, smoking status, and diabetes. 
 <br> <br>
Scores are generally categorized as low (<10%), moderate (10–20%), or high (>20%)
